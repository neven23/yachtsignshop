How It Works

From your first click to the sign on your hull.

1
Design or Upload
Use our configurator to pick your material, font, size and finish — or let the AI designer suggest combinations based on your boat's style. Prefer your own artwork? Upload a file and we'll cut it exactly.
2
We Send a Proof
Within 3 business days you receive a high-resolution digital proof showing exactly how your sign will look — full size, correct proportions, with your chosen finish rendered accurately. Review it on any device.
3
Approve or Revise
Reply with approval or request as many revisions as you need. We don't rush — the proof is free and unlimited changes are included. Production only starts when you're completely happy.
4
CNC Machined to Order
Your sign is CNC-cut from solid stock — naval brass, 316L stainless, or UV-stable acrylic — at ±0.1mm tolerance. Each letter is individually cut, deburred by hand, and polished to the finish you chose.
5
Ships in 7 Days
After approval, production and dispatch take 7 business days. We ship worldwide via DHL Express with full tracking. Your order includes a 1:1 installation template and mounting hardware.
6
Install in Under an Hour
The included template eliminates measuring. Tape it to your hull, drill pilot holes, remove, mount the sign. Our installation guide walks you through every step. Most customers finish in 20–40 minutes.
Common Questions
How long does the whole process take?
From order to delivery: typically 10–14 business days. Proof arrives within 3 days, production takes 7 days after approval, and shipping is 2–5 days depending on your location.
Can I change my mind after ordering?
Yes — you can make any changes during the proofing stage at no cost. Once you've approved and production has started, changes may incur a small fee. Contact us immediately and we'll do our best to accommodate.
What file formats can I upload?
SVG, AI, EPS, PDF, and high-resolution PNG or JPEG (300dpi+). Vector formats give the sharpest results. If you only have a low-resolution image, contact us — our design team can often redraw it.
Do you offer rush production?
Yes — contact us before ordering and we'll confirm availability. Rush orders (3–4 day production) incur a 40% surcharge. Not always possible during peak season.
