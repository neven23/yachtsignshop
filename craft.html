316L Stainless
01

316L Stainless Steel

Marine-grade 316L — the "L" is critical. It denotes low carbon content (≤0.03%), which prevents carbide precipitation along grain boundaries. The molybdenum content (2–3%) pushes the pitting resistance index above 25 — the minimum threshold for marine chloride environments.

Our steel carries a Mill Certificate with each batch confirming composition. We don't accept "stainless" without documentation. Neither should you.

C46400 Naval Brass
02

Naval Brass

C46400 naval brass — the same alloy specified for marine propeller shafts and through-hull fittings. The "naval" designation means it contains tin (0.75–1.25%), which suppresses dezincification — the process by which ordinary brass loses zinc ions in salt water, becomes porous, and ultimately fails.

Our brass arrives as flat stock, is sheared to blank, CNC-routed, then passed to our finishing team who cold-work the surface through four progressively finer grits before buffing to a mirror.

Production

From order to marina

Seven working days. Here's exactly what happens — not in vague marketing language, but in the actual sequence of operations.

1
Within 24 hours
Design Proof
A vectorised proof lands in your inbox. Exact lettering, correct scale, correct finish rendered. Approve or request changes — no limit, no charge.
2
Day 2
Material Prep
Sheet stock is sheared to blank. CNC program is generated from the approved vector. Toolpath is dry-run before any cutting begins.
3
Day 3
CNC Routing
24,000 RPM spindle, flood-cooled on metal. Climb-milling on the final pass gives a burr-free edge. Carbide tooling changed every 80 hours. ±0.1mm tolerance.
4
Day 4–5
Hand Polish
320 grit to remove machining marks. 600 to establish grain direction. 1200 to refine. Cotton wheel with compound to finish. ~20 minutes per sign, by hand, every time.
5
Day 5 (LED orders)
LED Wiring
Strip tested at full current for 2 hours before potting in marine epoxy. IP68-rated connector pre-wired with tinned cable. Tested again after potting.
6
Day 6
Inspection
Every sign photographed under raking light — the harshest way to reveal micro-scratches. Doesn't pass: re-polished from scratch. Not reworked. From scratch.
7
Day 7
Packed & Shipped
Foam-wrapped, double-wall corrugated, rigid corner protection. Drill template, stainless fixings, marine silicone included. Tracking number sent when label prints.
★
Every order
Installation Kit
1:1 paper drill template. Illustrated installation guide. 316 stainless stand-off fixings. Marine silicone compound. No upsell. No extra charge.
