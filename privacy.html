Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Applies to all visitors and customers
1. Who We Are

YachtSignShop operates yachtsignshop.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information from visitors and customers. If you have questions, contact us at hello@yachtsignshop.com.

2. What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to process and fulfil your order:

  • Name and email address — to send your design proof and order updates
  • Shipping address — to deliver your sign
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider; we never store card details
  • Sign specifications — name, font, material, finish, size, any custom notes
  • Order tracking queries — order number and email, used only to retrieve your order status

We do not collect data beyond what is needed to fulfil your order. We do not run advertising trackers or sell data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data
  • To manufacture and ship your sign
  • To send your design proof and any revision requests
  • To provide order status via our tracking system
  • To contact you about your order if we have a question
  • To handle any warranty or returns claims

We will not send you marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Data Storage & Security

Order data is stored securely in Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security. We do not store card numbers or CVV codes.

We apply 256-bit SSL encryption to all data transmitted through our website. Your connection to yachtsignshop.com is always secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate our business:

  • Netlify — website hosting (US-based)
  • Supabase — order database (US-based, SOC 2)
  • Google Fonts — font rendering (no personal data shared)
  • Shipping carriers — UPS, FedEx, or USPS to fulfil deliveries

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising tracking.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@yachtsignshop.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We use no analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking of any kind. The site may store minimal session data in your browser's local storage solely to maintain your design configuration during your visit.

8. Data Retention

We retain order data for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements. After this period, personal data is deleted. If you request earlier deletion, we will remove all data not required for legal compliance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will always display the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
