Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering a custom yacht sign.

Standard production is 5–7 business days, plus shipping. Rush orders (available at checkout for $95) ship in 3–5 business days. You'll receive a digital proof within 24 hours of ordering.

We use solid brass (not plated), 316-grade marine stainless steel, and UV-stable cast acrylic — all sourced for saltwater environments. Every sign is CNC-routed, not laser-etched or vinyl.

Yes — always. We email a high-resolution digital proof within 24 hours. Production only begins after you approve it. You can request unlimited revisions at no extra charge.

Standard widths range from 12" to 72" (30–183 cm). Custom sizes are available — contact us for a quote. Height is proportional to the letter style and averages 3–6 inches for most fonts.

All signs ship with pre-drilled mounting holes and stainless mounting hardware. Most installs take under 30 minutes with a drill and level. We include a step-by-step installation guide with every order.

Yes! LED halo lighting is available for brass and stainless signs. It adds a warm glow that makes your boat name visible at night. Pricing depends on sign width and letter count — toggle the option in the configurator to see your price. Learn more about our LED signs.

Yes — we ship worldwide. International shipping is calculated at checkout. Most international orders arrive within 7–14 business days via DHL Express. Import duties may apply depending on your country.

Because every sign is made to order, we can't accept returns for change-of-mind. However, if your sign arrives damaged or doesn't match your approved proof, we'll replace it at no cost — guaranteed.
